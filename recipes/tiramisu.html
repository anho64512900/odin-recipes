Tiramisu

This dessert is very fast and easy to prepare.

alt="tiramisu dessert"

Ingredients

  • 1 cup or 8 fl oz of heavy whipping cream
  • 1 cup or 8 fl oz of mascarpone cheese a room temperature
  • 1/3 cup of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 2 cups or 16 oz strong coffee or espresso at room temperature
  • 1 package of ladyfingers
  • cocoa powder for dusting the top of the dessert
  • 8x8 pan

Instructions

  1. Beat the whipping cream, sugar and vanilla until soft peaks form.
  2. Add the mascarpone cheese and whip the mixture until stiff peaks form
  3. Dip the ladyfingers once into the coffee.
  4. Line the coffee dipped ladyfingers into the 8x8 pan so that a layer is formed.
  5. Spread half the cream mixture over the ladyfinger layer.
  6. Repeat one more layer of coffee dipped ladyfingers over the cream layer.
  7. Spread the remaining cream layer over the second layer of ladyfingers.
  8. Dust the top of the dessert with the cocoa powder. A sieve can be used for dusting the cocoa powder.
  9. Refrigerate the dessert for 2 to 4 hours. Enjoy.
