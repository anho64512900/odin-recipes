Easy and Delicious Potato Dish

Ingredients

  • Food processor
  • Greased 10 inch round pan
  • 4 to 6 yukon gold potatoes
  • potato peeler
  • foil for the top of the pan
  • stick of butter
  • kosher salt
  • garlic is optional

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees Farenheit.
  2. peel potatoes and slice the potatoes using food processors 1/8 inches thick.
  3. If using garlic, chop until fine.
  4. Melt butter in a dish
  5. Layer the sliced potato rounds with garlic,salt and butter in the greased round dish
  6. There should be at least 4 layers
  7. Place the buttered side foil on top of the potatoes.
  8. Weigh down the potatoes by placing dried beans into a pie plate.
  9. Bake for 25 minutes.
  10. Uncover the potatoes and bake for approximately 20 more minutes until a knife can slice easily into the potatoes and the dish is golden brown.
  11. Serve in the buttered dish or unmold carefully and place on a cake stand.
